Our Story

Discover the people and passion behind SimpleSite

Who We Are

Founded in 2015, SimpleSite began as a small team of web enthusiasts with a big vision: to make website creation accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill.

Today, we've grown into a leading platform that empowers thousands of businesses, creators, and individuals to establish their online presence with beautiful, functional websites.

Our mission is to remove the complexity from web design while maintaining professional quality, so you can focus on what matters most - your content and your audience.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our company's history

Company Founded

June 2015

Three college friends launch SimpleSite from a garage with the goal of simplifying web design for non-technical users.

First Major Update

January 2017

Launch of drag-and-drop editor and mobile-responsive templates, revolutionizing our platform's capabilities.

Series A Funding

May 2018

Secured $5M in funding to expand our team and accelerate product development.

10,000 Customers

November 2020

Reached a major milestone with over 10,000 active customers using our platform worldwide.

Present Day

2023

Now serving over 25,000 customers with a team of 100+ passionate professionals across 8 countries.
